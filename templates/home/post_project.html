Plumar Crowdsource

We all have those moment when you dare need to get a project done within a tight timeline with very limited resources to hire talents or put a team together real quick. It’s always one of those days we want to avoid. We understand that running a business is hard and its even harder when closing deals and acquiring customers becomes a luxury because of limited resources and tight schedules. With a global pool of tech talents, Plumar Crowdsource offers you a platform to get your project done within your budget and preferred timeline.

How it works
  1. Submit your project requirements and propose your budget. Agree to Plumar Crowdsourceterms and conditions
  2. Plumar will review the viability of your project requirement and timeline.
  3. Your project will be matched to relevant team of tech talents to work on it within your stipulated timeline
  4. Schedule 1st and 2nd sprint demos with the team working on you project to review work done and test
  5. When the project is completed according to your requirements, you will be prompt for payment before final presentation and handover of the project to you
