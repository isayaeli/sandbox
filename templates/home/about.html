About Plumar

Plumar gives you wings to fly.
This is a safe space for startups to find talents that are eager to help them reach the sky.

Plumar is revolutionizing the talent space for startups by building exciting and productive teams to scale their businesses. We are driven by the vision of democratizing the hiring process to give startups an edge in finding top talents according to their terms, budget, and growth plan. Plumar is offering startups an opportunity to tell their stories to a global pool of talent that is looking for exciting and impactful companies to join. We understand that startups do not have deep pockets for hiring a workforce and may not be able to compete in talent acquisition with big corporations. This is why we have created PLUMAR, a safe space for startups to find talent that is eager to work with them in bringing their dreams to life. Startups can use an open source talent platform to find volunteers, full-timers, remote talents, advisors, consultants, or co-founders.We make hiring easy, affordable, and decentralized, to enable startups to find and work with talent from any part of the world. Through Plumar, we have opened up a world of possibilities for early-stage companies.

How we are revolutionizing the talent sourcing space for startups

Opportunity Listing:

Plumar offers startups the platform to list their available opportunities and be in front of top talents globally. Businesses now have the opportunity to attract the right talent who want to support or collaborate with startup companies to help them realize their vision.

Due Diligence:

To help startups recruit right on the first try, we offer an extra layer of vetting of talent through due diligence to validate their experience, skillset, passion, competencies, and domain knowledge. We present the due diligence report to the startup recruiter for better hiring decision-making. Startups review initial talents that match their opportunity, and then provide the top 3 candidates to Plumar for due diligence. Startups

Legal and Payroll

To enable startups to focus on their core business, Plumar offers legal and payroll services to startups. You choose your talents, while Plumar handles all the legal paperwork according to the talent's country of employment requirements. Plumar also manages payroll solutions and pays out wages in local currencies, freeing up startups and their employees to focus on growing the business.
